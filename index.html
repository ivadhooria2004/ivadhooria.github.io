Australian Visitors to Japan — Tourism Trend Analysis

Built in R using JNTO data 2003–2025 (AI-assisted). Trained a log-linear model (R² = 0.753) on pre-COVID years (2003–2019) and validated against the recovery period (2020–2025). 2025 actuals reached 1,058,396 visitors — +39% above the pre-COVID trend projection of 760,038 and +70% above the 2019 peak — a structural shift in Australian travel demand, not just a rebound.
